QR grades

It's a simple way to print qr code in your climbing gym to gather data about route grade assessment and user experience reviews.

New QR code generation and printing

  • Click on link above "New route (QR)
  • Fill in data: name of route, your nickname and preliminary route grade
  • Now you have two options:
    1. copy image and print it on your label printer
    2. copy route url and generate qr in label printer app
  • Once it's printed qr is going to forward you to route site.

How to add new route assessment

  • Scan QR code with your mobile
  • Fill in data: your nickname, your route grade and route assessment
  • Your nick will be saved as cookie for further inputs
