Climbing wall with QR codes
It's a simple way to gather info about routes on climbing walls with easily printed QR codes.

  • QR code is prepared for cheap label printers like Niimbot B1 for ca. 50$.
  • It's FREE to use, hobby project for enhance climbing experience.
  • It could be applied to climbing gym, climbing leagues through TAG system.
  • For support and feedback please join this whatsapp group
Easy QR code generation
QR generation is simplified to minimum. Most cheap label printers could print QR from Excel list.

  • Click on this link: new list (xlsx)
  • Download generated list in Excel
  • Print all the labels and stick it to route
  • Every QR code redirect you to individual route assessment site
  • During first input you state name, tag and primary grade.
Easy route review
Review process is minimalistic for quick assessments.

  • Scan QR code with your mobile
  • Fill in data: your nickname, your route grade and route assessment
  • Your username will be saved as cookie for further inputs
  • All data are stored on your personal site /user/user_id
Explore user data in many ways
There are insights you can get from gathered data, which can further optimisation of costs, e.g.:

  • What route grades are most popular?
  • When to set new routes on your gym?
  • What kind of routes are most liked?
  • Who sets the most liked routes?
