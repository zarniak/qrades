Routes management by qr codes
It's a simple way to manage routes on climbing walls with easily printed qr codes. Through easy qr code generation prepared for cheap label printers you can gather info about users route grade assessment and review. It's FREE to use, hobby project for enhance user climbing experience.
Easy QR code generation
QR generation is simplified to minimum. Most cheap label printers could print QR from Excel list.
  • Click on link above "New list (xlsx)"
  • Download generated list in Excel
  • Print all the labels and stick it to route
  • Every qr code redirect you to individual route assessment site
Easy route review
Voting is minimal input for quick assessments.
  • Scan QR code with your mobile
  • Fill in data: your nickname, your route grade and route assessment
  • Your nick will be saved as cookie for further inputs
